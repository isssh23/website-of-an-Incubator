Our Criteria

  • Operate with integrity, honesty, and transparency in all interactions with the incubator.
  • Abide by the terms and conditions set forth by the incubator, including the proper utilization of provided funds and space.
  • Respect the intellectual property and confidential information shared by the incubator and fellow incubatees.
  • Collaborate and share knowledge with other startups within the incubator community, fostering a supportive and inclusive environment.
  • Regularly communicate progress, challenges, and successes to the incubator management and actively seek their guidance and mentorship.
  • Participate in incubator events, workshops, and networking opportunities to maximize learning and growth.
  • Engage in fair competition and respect the ideas, innovations, and businesses of fellow incubatees.
  • Maintain a strong commitment to continuous learning and improvement, striving for personal and professional growth.
